Ziqi Zhang

PhD student at the Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School.

Tsinghua University

Ziqi Zhang is a PhD student at the Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School, specializing in the study of emerging environmental contaminants. Her research primarily focuses on identifying, quantifying, and understanding the pollution of emerging contaminants (ECs) and their impact on public health. Relevant findings have been published in Water Research, Environmental Pollution and SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT FOR RESOURCE-EFFICIENT SYSTEMS: CIRCULARITY IN ACTION. Her academic endeavors aim to develop water safety assurance strategies and sustainable environmental practices.
