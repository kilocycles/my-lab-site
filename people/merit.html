Merit HAASE

a double degree student between Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School and Leibniz University Hannover in Environmental Engineering.

Tsinghua University & Leibniz University Hannover

Merit Haase is a double degree student between Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School and Leibniz University Hannover in Environmental Engineering. Her current research focuses on emerging pollutants and their remediation in the environment by utilising and enhancing green-blue infrastructures. Furthermore she is an environmental engineer with a wide range of experiences in different sectors. Her current ambition is to gather knowledge about environmental solutions globally and help to lead into a sustainable future through a successful exchange of knowledge.
