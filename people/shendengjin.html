Dengjin Shen is a PhD student in Environmental Science and Engineering at Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School. His research interests include antibiotic resistance, microbiome, metagenomics, environmental microbiology, and applied microbiology. Currently, he is using experimental and genomics approaches to decipher the emergence and dessemination of antibiotic resistance by the excessive use of platicizers.
