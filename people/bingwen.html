Bingwen Liu

PhD candidate in Environmental Science and Engineering at Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School

Tsinghua University

Bingwen Liu is a PhD candidate in Environmental Science and Engineering at Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School. Her research interest lies in the transmission and evolution of antimicrobial resistance in microbial communities within wastewater treatment plants. She is currently using metagenomic and metatranscriptomic approaches to investigate the occurrence and transmission of antibiotic resistance genes in aquatic environment.
