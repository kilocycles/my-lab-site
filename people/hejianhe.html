Jianhe He is a master's student at the Institute of Environment and Ecology of Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School. His research focuses on hydrology and the application of machine learning in environmental engineering. He uses hydrological models such as SWAT in combination with machine learning to simulate and study the changes in river pollution and provide management suggestions.
