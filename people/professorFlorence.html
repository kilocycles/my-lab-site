Jong Mui Choo, Florence (Ph.D.)

Assistant Professor of Environmental Science & Engineering,PI of Blue Health Research Lab

Tsinghua University

Dr. Florence joined Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School (Tsinghua SIGS) in October 2022 as an Assistant Professor and Ph.D. advisor. Prior to this, she was a postdoctoral researcher at the National University of Singapore (2019–2022), where her work focused on interdisciplinary research in environment, public health, and ecological health. Her research interests include emerging contaminants in coastal, riverine, and estuarine environments (e.g., microplastics, organic pollutants, antibiotics, and antibiotic-resistant microbes), ecotoxicology, environmental risk assessment, and energy-efficient wastewater treatment technologies for water safety and pollution control. Her work aims to protect ecosystems and human health while offering practical insights and solutions to pressing environmental challenges.

Interests
  • Antimicrobial resistance (AMR)
  • Microplastics
  • Other synthetic organic contaminants (e.g., plastic additives, liquid crystal monomers)
