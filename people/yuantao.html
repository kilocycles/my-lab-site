Tao Yuan

Research Assistant at Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School.

TaoYuan is a research assistant at Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School. His research interests include 1) removal of antibiotic resistant bacteria and antibiotic resistance genes; 2) Emerging contaminants degradation by environmental functional materials combined with advanced oxidation process; 3) prediction of environmental pollutants based on machine learning. Currently, he is investigating single atom catalyst activated persulfate to remove ESBL-producing bacteria and antibiotic resistance genes.
