Bangguo Wang

PhD candidate in Environmental Science and Engineering at Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School.

Tsinghua University

Bangguo Wang is a PhD candidate in Environmental Science and Engineering at Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School. His research interests include 1) occurrence, transfer, and fate of antibiotics resistance genes in the marine environment; 2) Developing nanobubble-based strategies for efficient and sustainable degradation of organic pollutants. Currently, he is investigating effects of emerging pollutants on the antibiotics resistance genes within the phycosphere niche.
